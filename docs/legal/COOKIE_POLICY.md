# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

YatinVeda uses cookies to enhance your experience, keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our Service.

## Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important purposes:

1. **Essential Functions**: Enable core features like authentication and security
2. **Performance**: Help us understand how the Service is performing
3. **User Experience**: Remember your preferences and settings
4. **Security**: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
5. **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns to improve the Service

## Types of Cookies We Use

### 1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `access_token` | User authentication | 30 minutes | Session |
| `refresh_token` | Token renewal | 30 days | Persistent |
| `csrf_token` | CSRF protection | Session | Session |
| `session_id` | Session management | Session | Session |
| `mfa_verified` | MFA verification status | Session | Session |

**What they do:**
- Keep you logged in as you navigate
- Verify that you are who you say you are
- Protect against cross-site request forgery attacks
- Manage your session securely

**Legal basis:** Strictly necessary for service delivery (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

### 2. Functional Cookies (Can be Disabled)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `user_preferences` | UI preferences (theme, language) | 1 year | Persistent |
| `timezone` | User timezone setting | 1 year | Persistent |
| `cookie_consent` | Your cookie preferences | 1 year | Persistent |
| `locale` | Language preference | 1 year | Persistent |
| `currency_preference` | Preferred currency display | 1 year | Persistent |

**What they do:**
- Remember your language preference
- Save your theme choice (light/dark mode)
- Store your timezone for accurate time displays
- Remember your cookie consent choices

**Legal basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

### 3. Analytics Cookies (Can be Disabled)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `_analytics_session` | Track unique visits | Session | Session |
| `_usage_metrics` | Usage statistics | 30 days | Persistent |
| `_performance` | Performance monitoring | 7 days | Persistent |

**What they do:**
- Count page visits and traffic sources
- Measure feature usage and engagement
- Identify performance bottlenecks
- Track error rates and issues

**Important Notes:**
- Self-hosted analytics (Prometheus/Grafana)
- Data stays on our servers
- No third-party tracking
- Anonymized data collection
- Aggregated reporting only

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest or Consent (depending on jurisdiction)

### 4. Security Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies protect against malicious activity.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `device_fingerprint` | Device recognition | 90 days | Persistent |
| `trusted_device` | Trusted device marker | 90 days | Persistent |
| `rate_limit` | Rate limiting tracker | 1 hour | Session |
| `security_token` | Additional verification | Session | Session |

**What they do:**
- Detect suspicious login attempts
- Remember trusted devices for MFA
- Prevent brute force attacks
- Rate limit API requests

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest in security (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

## Cookies We DO NOT Use

YatinVeda currently does not use:

- ❌ **Advertising Cookies**: We don't show third-party ads
- ❌ **Social Media Tracking Cookies**: No Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, etc.
- ❌ **Cross-Site Tracking**: We don't track you across other websites
- ❌ **Third-Party Marketing Cookies**: No data brokers or marketing platforms

## Cookie Duration

- Deleted when you close your browser
- Used for temporary data (authentication, CSRF protection)

### Persistent Cookies

- Used for preferences and settings
- You can delete them manually anytime

| Duration | Purpose |

## How to Manage Cookies

### In YatinVeda Settings

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings → Privacy
3. Manage cookie preferences
4. Save your choices

**Options:**
- ✅ Essential Cookies (Always Active)
- ⬜ Functional Cookies (Optional)
- ⬜ Analytics Cookies (Optional)

### In Your Browser

All browsers allow you to manage cookies:

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
2. See all cookies and site data
3. Remove or block specific cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Options → Privacy & Security
2. Cookies and Site Data
3. Manage Data or Clear Data

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Manage Website Data
3. Remove or block cookies

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Privacy, search, and services
2. Cookies and site permissions
3. Manage and delete cookies

**Browser Links:**
- [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge Cookie Settings](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### Browser Privacy Mode

**Private/Incognito Mode:**
- Automatically deletes cookies when closed
- Doesn't sync preferences across sessions
- Requires login each time
- More privacy, less convenience

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

### Disabling Essential Cookies
⚠️ **The Service will not work properly**
- Cannot log in
- Cannot access protected features
- Security features disabled
- Session management broken

### Disabling Functional Cookies
⚠️ **Reduced convenience**
- Settings not remembered
- Must re-select language each visit
- Theme preference resets
- Timezone settings lost

### Disabling Analytics Cookies
✅ **Minimal impact**
- Full Service functionality maintained
- We can't improve based on your usage
- Help us less with bug identification

## Cookies and Personal Data

### Anonymous Cookies
Most analytics cookies contain no personal data:
- Randomly generated IDs
- Aggregated statistics
- No email, name, or identifying info

### Cookie with Personal Data
Essential and security cookies may contain:
- User ID (not personally identifiable alone)

**Protection measures:**
- Encrypted transmission (TLS 1.3)
- Secure and HttpOnly flags
- SameSite protection
- Short expiration times
- Token rotation
